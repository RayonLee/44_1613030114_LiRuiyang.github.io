Ourselves

Self Introduction

In 2016, we just entered the university. We were assigned to the same class under the combination of fate. She is from Hunan while I am from Shandong. The distance of nearly a thousand kilometers is suddenly getting just so close, so close that I noticed the cute girl in the first day.

It is funny that our first year of college life provided us much more colorful group activities than in the years to come. We did not go through each other, even not an in-depth conversation. I have never thought that the girl I can see every day in this class will have brought unprecedented changes to my mentality and given me this special experience that I never had.

In the second year of the university, we gradually became more connected. I only found out that this girl around me has so many similarities with me. She knew me, cared about me, and our distance was getting smaller. Finally in the third year of college, we started this relationship.
